Abigail Ng

I am a computer science major in the University of Michigan's College of Engineering in the class of 2023. I am also taking a creative writing minor to grow in my hobby. I have internship experience in front-end web development and front-end mobile development, as well as class and project experience in full-stack web development, computer security, compilers, and operating systems.

I always like to say that computer science is an inherently creative career. Being a computer scientist requires the developer to come up with unique solutions for every single thing they develop. This is why I greatly value my hobbies aside from my computer science skills. I enjoy crocheting, knitting, drawing, writing, sewing, and more.
